Who to serves? 
Glow Up is a beauty and wellness app designed to meet the needs of a wide range of individuals. The program is open to both men and women, regardless of their age, gender, or background, who are passionate about self-care and personal growth. It is developed to meet the needs of a wide range of users, including beauty enthusiasts seeking expert advice, busy professionals in search of convenient solutions, or individuals who have specific questions regarding hair, skin, weight, nails, or mental health issues. The "Glow Up" app provides personalized recommendations and resources to empower people from all walks of life to take control of their health and embark on a transformative journey toward better health.

APP Description: 
Glow up app, the program system provides a chat bot system as a mentor through all your GLOW UP experience, We Designed a program in the field of self-care that allows the user to write down their problems, for example, hair loss, weight gain, or any other issue. The chatbot will communicate with the users and give them solutions to their situations, and the chatbot will motivate the user constantly, providing him comfort and courage.
